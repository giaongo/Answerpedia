What we do

Peope appreciate and never forget that helping hand especially when times are tough - Pulsifer Catherine.

Pulsifer and we share the same mission: to help others in need. In the current fast-paced world, it is convenient to just focus on our own problems, deal with our own miseries.

For us - Winter is coming team - we found joy and happiness in delivering help, assitance to those in need whenever we can, physically or virtually. That is why we came up with Answerpedia, a website that connects people through system of questions and answers. A website that people can share their knowlegde, give a helping hand wherever and whenever.
